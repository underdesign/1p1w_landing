What We Don't Do

These are touted as benefits, or offer a more slick looking package, but at a cost of slower load times, more overhead, useless code libraries, and more.

No Useless Animation

Animation without intent is distraction. Flying boxes and shifting icons only distract readers from the copy that will sell them.

No Bloat

Checked and Verified HTML, then it's optimized & streamlined for the host. Support graphics are condensed into sprites for quicker load and less web requests. No single-use scenario code left embedded. No jQuery, which we find to be bloated Javascript library for lazy programmers

Copy > Pictures

You know that Google doesn't direct traffic to your site due to pictures you use or colors you choose. Often, using copyrighted images will get a cease-and-decist order, or a demand for copyright payments.

No Menus

Why sub-divide your limited copy and benefits across multiple pages, just to increase clicks, or simply frustrate your users who don't know where you put that bit of information? Do your visitor the favor by answering the obvious above the fold, but keeping all your info in one place.

No Search

Limited content and page scope means it won't get lost in the millions of other sites out there. A well-tuned static site only grows in search reputation the longer it stays around.

No Closed Source

Each job is a public (but copyrighted) repository on GitHub, to allow simple backup and changes. We write standards compliant HTML5, check it thoroughly, and use open-source code as available.
